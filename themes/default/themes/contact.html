Contact Us

The contact form included with the "Barely Corporate" theme is souped up with a bit of jQuery and ajax. If you forget to fill in any of the fields you'll see a simple error message telling you what you did wrong. Once all fields are filled in correctly, the form is submited without the browser needing to refresh. A simple little PHP script that you configure will shoot the message to you in an email. Go ahead and play with the form a bit. Experience the simplistic awesomeness.
